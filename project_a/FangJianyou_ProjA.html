Walking Creatures

  • On the canvas are a crawling spider and a mysterious walking creature(we call it MWC).
  • Drag your mouse on the canvas to change spider's posture.
  • Hold z key and drag mouse to change MWC's posture
  • Click your mouse anywhere on the canvas to let MWC go to where you click.
  • Hit space to pause motion
  • Use arrow keys to move MWC
Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
